About Us

Ashwa Racing is a Formula Student team based at R.V. College of Engineering, Bangalore competing in Formula Student competitions all over the world. Our team consists of passionate engineering students working together to design, build, and race prototypes under formula student regulations.

Founded in 2005, our team has grown to include over 200 dedicated members across multiple disciplines including mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering. We strive for innovation and excellence in automotive engineering.

Formula Student competitions challenge teams to develop a small formula-style racing prototype which is evaluated for its potential as a production item. Through this process, students gain invaluable experience in design, manufacturing, and teamwork.

Our achievements include multiple podium finishes in national competitions and recognition for innovation in electric vehicle technology. We continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in student motorsport.
